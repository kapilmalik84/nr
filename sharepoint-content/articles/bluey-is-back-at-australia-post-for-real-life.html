Bluey is back at Australia Post – for real life

Dust off your coin purses Bluey fans — Bluey is back at Australia Post with the very first $2 Bluey Dollarbuck coin collection.

Struck by the Royal Australian Mint in partnership with BBC Studios, the first-of-their-kind $2 coins feature Bluey alongside her family and friends. The fun and charming illustrations, designed in collaboration with the team at Ludo Studio, showcase moments from several of Bluey's much-loved episodes — including Camping and Granny Mobile — and come as standard uncirculated coins, retailing from $20.

Australia Post Executive General Manager Retail, Brand & Marketing, Josh Bannister encouraged fans to visit their local Post Office and join the Bluey fun.

"Bluey is loved by families across Australia and the world, and these first ever $2 coins are a fun way for fans to connect with the characters they love," Mr Bannister said.

Bluey fans can visit participating Post Offices or online from today (6 May) to buy individual coins for $20 each and the full coin tube and folder set for $34.

For more information visit auspost.com.au/shop/collectables/bluey-collectables

About Bluey

The series follows Bluey, a loveable, inexhaustible, Blue Heeler dog, who lives with her Mum, Dad and her little sister, Bingo.

Bluey is produced by Ludo Studio for ABC KIDS (Australia) and co-commissioned by ABC Children's and BBC Studios Kids & Family.
