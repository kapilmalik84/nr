01 — ANALYSIS

Where the Newsroom stands today

The Newsroom is functionally complete but visually disconnected from the flagship auspost.com.au experience. It reads as a default AEM EDS theme rather than an Australia Post property. Five gaps drive the redesign.

1No brand expression
Flat white header, system-default type rhythm, and a thin red band that doesn't carry the confident red-hero identity of the main site. The logo lockup and "Newsroom" wordmark feel bolted on.
2Broken / empty blocks
A stray red "Read more" panel sits with no heading or context, and the EDS edit toolbar bleeds into the hero. Content hierarchy collapses below the fold.
3Weak article system
The "Latest" grid has no category filtering, inconsistent card heights, and a meta line (date · category) that's hard to scan. No featured-story treatment beyond the lead.
4Inconsistent components
Buttons, radii, and link styles vary between sections. "Quick links" uses chevron rows while other lists use arrows — no single component vocabulary.
What the reference gives us
Confident red hero
A full-bleed red field with an overlapping rounded white card — instantly "Australia Post".
Pill geometry
Fully-rounded buttons, large-radius cards, soft shadows, generous whitespace.
Editorial cards
Photo-led tiles with arrow affordances and a clear category → headline → action rhythm.

Australia Post to deliver its 25 millionth Connection Postcard with Beyond Blue

Australia Post Media Team
07 May 2026 · 3 min read
inX
Article hero image · 16:9

Four million prepaid Connection Postcards are being delivered to letterboxes across the country, as research reveals loneliness is causing distress for one in three Australians.

As Beyond Blue celebrates 25 years of supporting Australians' mental health, Australia Post is helping spread connection the analogue way — through the simple act of sending a card to someone you care about.

The Connection Postcards are available free at participating Post Offices and can be sent anywhere in Australia at no cost, removing every barrier to reaching out.

"A handwritten note can be the moment that reminds someone they're not alone."

Since the program launched, more than 24 million postcards have travelled across the country — and the 25 millionth marks a milestone in one of the nation's largest community wellbeing initiatives.

WCAG 2.1 AA checklist
✓Contrast
Red #DC1928 on white = 5.3:1. White on red hero = 5.3:1. Body #44444C on white = 9.1:1. All pass AA.
✓Keyboard & focus
Category tabs use role=tablist + arrow keys. Visible 3px focus ring on every interactive element.
✓Semantics
Cards are <article> with a single <a> wrapping the heading; one H1 per page; landmark regions.
✓Target size & motion
All controls ≥ 44px. Hover lift respects prefers-reduced-motion. Alt text required on authored images.
